# 24-hour Total Rainfall Accumulation Scale (PAGASA)

According to an official statement, PAGASA (2019) defines the following 24-hour (daily) accumulated rainfall threshold values:

* **Light Rain**
    * **Less than 60 mm** accumulated over 24 hours.
        * This volume does not indicate a regional flooding threat.
* **Moderate Rain**
    * **60 to 180 mm** accumulated over 24 hours.
        * This volume indicates that localized flooding is expected.
* **Heavy Rain**
    * **More than 180 mm** accumulated over 24 hours.
        * This volume indicates that widespread, severe flooding is highly probable.
